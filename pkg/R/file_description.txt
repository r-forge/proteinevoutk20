simtrees2.RData : trees used for simulations. It includes 6 trees, 2 trees for 4 tips, 8 tips and 12 tips. Of the 2 trees, one has comb topology and one has symmetric topology. For the trees with 12 tips, the topology is random.

These trees are stored in the file: simtrees.txt

pphyproevo.R: main R script, including functions to set up model, to find MLEs.

simulation.R: based on pphyproevo.R, the script to do simulations in a time interval or on a tree.
